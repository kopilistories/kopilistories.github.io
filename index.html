About the Film

Set against the landscapes of rural Assam, Kaldia is a quiet mystery where scientific curiosity and ancient memory begin to converge. As unusual celestial events and unexplained seismic activity unsettle an entire region, forgotten places and fragments of the past slowly reveal that some stories are older than history itself.

About KOPILI STORIES

KOPILI STORIES is an independent film studio based in Assam, India, dedicated to creating thoughtful cinema rooted in landscape, memory and human curiosity. Our films draw inspiration from mythology, science, oral traditions and the unseen connections between people, place and time.
