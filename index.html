কালদিয়া

Short Film

Set against the landscapes of rural Assam, Kaldiya is a quiet mystery where scientific curiosity and ancient memory begin to converge. As unusual celestial events and unexplained seismic activity unsettle the region, forgotten places and fragments of the past slowly suggest that some stories may be older than history itself. At the centre of it all is Kachpur, a remote place in the lower Brahmaputra valley, where something seems to connect the present to a much older and largely forgotten past. What remains, what has been lost, and what may still lie hidden beneath familiar stories form part of the mystery that Kaldiya only begins to uncover. Conceived as the first part of a possible trilogy, Kaldiya opens the door to a larger story that will gradually move deeper into the history, people and mythology surrounding Kachpur. Science remains at its core, but so do people, memory and the ways in which extraordinary events can shape a culture long after their origins have been forgotten. Rooted in the lower Brahmaputra valley and the Himalayan plains of Assam, Kaldiya is ultimately a human story, told through a landscape of rivers, forests, villages and memories, where the boundary between what is known, what is remembered, and what is believed is not always clear.

About

Our films are guided not by genre, but by the pursuit of genuine human emotion. Whether inspired by everyday life, history, myth, or imagination, we strive to create cinema with patience, honesty, and artistic integrity.
